/04 — THE SECOND BRAIN

The Vault is the company.

Everything we know — every client, every SOP, every pattern — lives in a markdown Vault called the Second Brain. It's version-controlled, AI-readable, and it's how Amplio scales.

We use the IPARAG system: seven top-level folders, each with one job. When in doubt about where something goes, the folder number tells you.

/05 — KARPATHY'S THREE LAYERS

Raw → Wiki → Schema.

The Vault is built on a 3-layer architecture inspired by Karpathy's LLM Wiki. Understand this and you understand how the system learns.

LAYER 01

Raw Sources

00-Inbox/raw/

Immutable inputs. Articles, transcripts, PDFs, clippings. Clipped in. Never edited. Tagged processed: true once compiled.

LAYER 02

The Wiki

05-Galaxy/ + 03-Resources/

Compiled, structured, interlinked. Atomic Galaxy notes + Resources. This is what Claude reads when answering a question.

LAYER 03

The Schema

CLAUDE.md + program.md

The rules. How the Vault works, what gets routed where, what Claude should read on-demand. The brain of the brain.

Compile. Lint. Enrich.

Step 01

Capture

Raw material hits `00-Inbox/raw/`.

Step 02

Compile

Extract concepts, create/update Galaxy notes.

Step 03

Link

Wikilinks to hubs. No orphans allowed.

Step 04

Lint

Broken links, missing summaries, stale content.

Step 05

Enrich

Autoresearch refreshes thin notes with fresh findings.

Full loop documented in 03-Resources/Playbooks/vault-autoresearch-playbook.md.

/07 — DAILY RHYTHM

How a day actually looks.

Morning brief. Deep work in blocks. Async-first. The Vault stays alive because every session leaves it richer than it started.

Morning — "Session brief"+
Open Claude, say "Session brief". Get the state of the world — what changed in memory, what's open on your desk, what's next in the queue. Takes 30 seconds. Sets your direction.
Deep work blocks+
Async chat with clients. Build tracking. Review campaigns. Draft outreach. Write playbooks. Every output goes in the right Vault folder — not Slack, not Drive, not your desktop.
After every client interaction+
Two rituals: (1) Extract client signals into their _overview.md's ## Client Signals section. (2) If you made a recommendation, log it in their recommendations-tracker.md. This is how the system learns.
Email discipline — "update_" label+
Any email about a client with "update_" in the subject gets labeled "Amplio Updates" in Gmail. Processing that email = syncing the Vault: client overview, project notes, pipeline dashboard, and index all get updated.
End of day — "Update memory"+
Before you close the laptop, run "Update memory". Claude overwrites the CURRENT STATE and appends today's log entry. Tomorrow-you starts fast because of it.
Scheduled autoresearch+
Background tasks run on a schedule — compile, lint, enrich, pattern analysis, vault health scoring. Check 03-Resources/Playbooks/scheduled-tasks-reference.md for the full list.
