Onboarding Checklist

Triggered by: "New client: [name]"

  • Create client folder at 06-Clients/[name]/
  • Fill _overview.md with client details, contacts, pricing, timeline
  • Create subfolders with _index.md in each: meeting-notes/, reports/, campaigns/, tracking/, correspondence/
  • Update _Client-Index.md with new client record
  • Build data context (skill: vault-autoresearch:client-data-context) → auto-creates data-context.md
  • Create recommendations-tracker.md from template
  • Create project in 01-Projects/Active/ if time-bound work
  • Run onboarding intelligence pre-loader (seeds applicable cross-client patterns)
  • Set up platform access: GA4, GTM, Google Ads, CRM, email (Klaviyo, Braze, etc.)

All new clients automatically get health score tracking, signal extraction, and recommendation tracking.

Client Health Scoring Model

Six weighted dimensions. 100 points total. Triggers: <70 = watch, <50 = critical alert.

Data Quality
20 points
Clean, complete data from all connected platforms (GA4, Ads, etc.)
Engagement
20 points
Recency & frequency of meaningful contact (meetings, email, check-ins)
Recommendations
20 points
% of our advice acted on; adoption rate; velocity to action
Performance Trend
20 points
Are their key metrics improving? Conversions, ROAS, traffic growth?
Contract Health
10 points
Payment status, invoice delays, tier, renewal date proximity
Vault Completeness
10 points
Do we have a complete picture? _overview, data-context, signals, recs, notes?
THRESHOLDS
85–100: Healthy — proactive engagement
70–84: Watch — light concerns, schedule check-in
50–69: At Risk — escalate, weekly monitoring
0–49: Critical — immediate action required

Client Signals: Extraction & Tracking

Signals are extracted from all client comms (meetings, emails, updates) and logged in _overview.md under ## Client Signals.

Format: [YYYY-MM-DD] [CODE] — Description (source: email/meeting/update)

FR
Feature Request
"Client asked about TikTok tracking"
OB
Objection/Pushback
"Disagreed with attribution model"
SAT+
Satisfaction (Positive)
"Loved the monthly report design"
SAT-
Satisfaction (Negative)
"Frustrated with onboarding timeline"
PS
Priority Shift
"Moving budget from Google to Meta"
EX
Expansion Signal
"Asked about CRM integration"
CH
Churn Signal
"Mentioned reviewing agency spend"
DA
Data/Access Signal
"Finally granted GTM access"
IC

Signals feed into pattern library. Three identical signals from different clients = new playbook candidate.

Monthly Report Template

Every report is scored 0–100. Refine weakest section until ≥90, then ship.

1. Executive Summary
2–3 sentences. Headline first. Lead with wins or risks.
2. KPI Dashboard
Table: Metric | This Month | Last Month | Change % | Target | Status ✓/✗
3. Channel Performance
Google Ads, Meta, Others (TikTok, email, organic). Mini breakdown tables.
4. Key Wins
3 specific, quantified achievements this month.
5. Areas for Improvement
Table: Area | Issue | Root Cause | Recommendation | Timeline
6. Recommendations & Next Steps
Immediate (this week) + Medium-term (1–3 months). Logged to recommendations-tracker.
7. Appendix
Segment breakdown, audience insights, platform-specific tracking notes.
QUALITY LOOP
Draft → Score (0–100) → Refine weakest section → Re-score → Ship at ≥90

Recommendations Tracker

Every recommendation we make is logged to the client's recommendations-tracker.md. This is how the system learns what works.

ID Date Recommendation Pattern ID Priority Status Outcome
REC-001 2026-03-15 Enable server-side GTM for GA4 GTM-001 High Actioned Pending measurement
REC-002 2026-03-22 Add email domain authentication EMAIL-AUTH-001 Medium Measured Email deliverability +12%
Status Workflow
Pending: Not yet acted on · Follow-up flag at 14+ days
Actioned: Client confirmed implementation · Check for outcome at 14+ days
Measured: Data collected · Outcome assigned
Closed: Outcome logged, pattern updated, learning complete

Outcomes feed into cross-client pattern library. Patterns with 3+ positive outcomes become playbooks.

Communication Guidelines

From Amplio's brand voice guide. Apply to all client-facing and internal comms.

Sound like this
  • Smart, experienced colleague
  • Confident, not arrogant
  • Lead with proof & specifics
  • Direct, not blunt
  • Technical, not intimidating
  • Warm, not casual
  • Professional but human
Use these words
Do use:
Fix · Solve · Prove · Embed · Automate · Measure
Don't use:
Optimize · Enhance · Deploy · Leverage · Synergize · Robust
Golden rule
Specific numbers over vague qualifiers always. Not "increased significantly" but "+23% lift." Not "better results" but "+$4,200 incremental revenue."

Quick Reference: Commands

These commands trigger full workflows in the vault system.

"New client: [name]"
Creates folder at 06-Clients/[name]/, fills _overview, subfolders, index, updates _Client-Index.
"Client brief: [name]"
Reads _overview.md + recent notes. Returns 1-paragraph status summary.
"Meeting notes: [client]"
Creates dated note in meeting-notes/ folder. Signals extracted automatically.
"Pipeline review"
Summarizes all prospects. Scores, stages, next actions, win probability.
"Client health"
Runs health scoring for all active clients. Table with scores, trends, alerts.
"Track recommendation: [client]"
Adds row to client's recommendations-tracker.md. Requires: text, pattern ID, priority.
"Learning report"
Summarizes: recs made/actioned/measured, pattern movements, signals extracted, health changes.
"Client signals: [name]"
Reads and summarizes all signals from _overview.md. Trend analysis included.
"Compile the vault"
Reads all unprocessed raw sources in 00-Inbox/raw/. Extracts concepts, creates Galaxy notes, cross-links.
