Client Signals System

Extract qualitative signals from every client communication. Log in _overview.md `## Client Signals` section. Use to refine health scoring and catch churn early.

Positive Signal
"We're impressed with the monthly insights. How can we deepen the partnership?"
Neutral / Inquiry
"Can we adjust our reporting cadence to bi-weekly instead of monthly?"
Churn Risk Signal
"Budget constraints. Considering cutting back data support. Can we reduce scope?"
Feature Request
"Need custom dashboard for leadership. Real-time daily updates."
Objection / Concern
"Data isn't moving the needle on our campaigns. Worried we're not seeing ROI."
Win Signal
"Tracking fix recovered 12k in lost conversions last month. Renewing for 12 months."

Monthly Reporting Cadence

Consistent, predictable reporting rhythm builds trust and creates momentum for follow-up conversations.

Week 1 of Month N+1: Data Prep

Extract final data from GA4, ads, CRM. Validate accuracy. Flag anomalies.

Week 2 of Month N+1: Report Build

Generate dashboard. Write narrative (wins, risks, next steps). Prepare talking points.

Week 2-3 of Month N+1: Delivery

Email report. Schedule 30-min walkthrough call with primary contact. Confirm receipt.

Week 3-4 of Month N+1: Follow-up

Address questions. Log recommendations in tracker. Extract signals. Update health score.

Monthly Report Template

Each report follows this structure: Executive Summary > Key Metrics > Deep Dive > Recommendations > Appendix.

Executive Summary

1–2 sentences. Headline result. Traffic, conversions, ROAS, or tracking health status.

Key Metrics

4–6 tiles. Month-over-month comparison. Sessions, users, conversions, ROAS, CAC, LTV. Show trend arrows.

Deep Dive

2–3 narratives. Traffic sources, user behavior, conversion funnel, audience segments. What changed? Why?

Recommendations

3–5 actionable. Optimize ad spend, fix tracking gaps, test landing pages, expand segments. Include expected lift.

Appendix

Detailed tables, segment breakdowns, ad performance, methodology notes.

Escalation Paths

Health score drops, churn signals spike, or technical emergencies. Know your escalation playbook.

Tier 1: Health Score 70–75
Outreach Call

30-min check-in. Ask: "How are we doing? Any concerns?" Listen for signals. Refresh value prop if needed.

Tier 2: Health Score 60–69
Executive Win-Back

Involve Walter or senior advisor. Identify root cause (scope, delivery, ROI, relationship). Propose concrete fix or renewal negotiation.

Tier 3: Health Score <60 or Churn Notice
Churn Management

Exit agreement. Offboard gracefully. Document learnings. Preserve referral channel if possible.

Technical Emergency
Incident Response

Tracking failure, data loss, or data gap. Resolve within 24h. Provide status updates every 4h. Root-cause analysis + prevention plan within 48h.

Communication Voice & Guidelines

Every client-facing communication reflects Amplio's brand. Helpful, jargon-light, outcome-focused.

Outcome-Focused
"This tracking fix should recover ~12k in lost conversions. Let's test it this week."
Clear Over Clever
"Sessions are 23% down because Google updated Chrome's privacy rules. It's affecting everyone."
Proactive, Not Reactive
"Before you make this budget shift, we should model the impact on data quality. Can we talk first?"
Partner, Not Vendor
"What would success look like for you in Q2? Let's align on the metrics that matter."
Specific, Not Vague
"Run this test for 2 weeks on 30% of paid traffic. We'll know by April 15 if it lifts ROAS."
Admit Unknowns
"We don't have that data yet. Here's what we can measure instead, and why it matters."
