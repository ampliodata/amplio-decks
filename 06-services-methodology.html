Delivery principles

The six rules we don't bend.

01

Ship a foundation before anything clever

No attribution models on broken tracking. Fix the basics first, every time.

02

Document like we're going to disappear

Every container, every event, every pipeline has a Loom and a doc. No tribal knowledge.

03

One senior, not a team of juniors

Clients talk to the person doing the work. No account managers between the expertise and the problem.

04

AI does the repetition, not the thinking

Claude & automation scale the dull work. Judgement calls stay human.

05

Report on outcomes, not activity

Reports show ROAS movement, not a list of tickets closed.

06

Fire fast, refund faster

If we're not the right fit we'll say so. 30-day Foundation warranty is non-negotiable.
