11 / Outreach Framework

How to Start Conversations

Cold Outreach
Under 100 words. Structure: Hook → Problem → Proof → CTA (soft)
Warm Follow-up
Under 150 words. Reference the introduction or prior conversation.
Discovery Questions by Persona
Agency Owner "What's your biggest bottleneck when onboarding new clients with tracking?"
Head of Performance "How often do your attribution numbers differ between GA4 and your ad platforms?"
CMO "How confidently can you tie marketing spend to pipeline in your next board review?"
E-commerce Manager "Are you losing conversions due to incomplete data collection?"
Startup Founder "What's currently blocking you from understanding your full user funnel?"
Best Channels
LinkedIn DM: Agency Owner, Startup Founder
Email: Head of Performance, E-commerce Manager
Warm intro: CMO, Enterprise decision-makers
12 / Brand Voice

How We Sound

Sound like a smart, experienced colleague who genuinely wants to help you succeed.

Confident, Not Arrogant
Do: "We've lifted ROAS by 23% on average"
Don't: "We're the best data experts"
Direct, Not Blunt
Do: "Your GA4 isn't tracking purchases correctly—here's why"
Don't: "Your setup is broken"
Technical, Not Intimidating
Do: "Server-side GTM isolates conversion data from third-party cookies"
Don't: "You need sGTM asap"
Warm, Not Casual
Do: "Let's fix this tracking gap together"
Don't: "Yo, ur tracking is sus"
Power Verbs
fix solve prove embed automate
optimize enhance deploy leverage synergize

Golden rule: Specific numbers over vague qualifiers. Always.
