CRM

The Attio blueprint.

Three core objects. One pipeline. Clear rules about what belongs where. The attio-crm-enrichment skill enforces the blueprint every time we add a record.

P

People

  • first_name, last_name, email
  • linkedin, title, seniority
  • persona (from ICP skill)
  • company (ref)
  • last_touch, next_step
C

Companies

  • name, domain, country
  • tracking_score (from scraper)
  • cmp_status, gtm_status
  • opportunity_tier (high/mid/low)
  • agency_type, industry
D

Deals

  • name, stage, amount, tier
  • company (ref), primary_contact (ref)
  • source (direct / agency / referral)
  • close_date, owner
  • lost_reason (when closed)
