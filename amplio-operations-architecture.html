FEEDBACK & VALIDATION

Learning Layer

Five feedback loops close the gap between recommendations and measured outcomes.

1

Recommendation Outcome Check

Actioned >14 days → check data for measurable result → Measured status

2

Client Signal Extraction

Categories: Feature Requests, Objections, Satisfaction, Pressure, Expansion, Churn, Data Appetite, Implementation

3

Client Health Scoring

0–100 composite per client across 6 dimensions. Scores <70 trigger escalation alerts.

4

Pattern Contradiction Testing

Three contradictions in 30 days → auto-downgrade confidence in pattern

5

Data Context Staleness

Manual contexts >30 days → flag for refresh. API-connected sources always fresh.

EXPERIMENTATION FRAMEWORK

Ad Campaign Autoresearch

Certify Education Google Ads. Escalating risk levels with automated execution guardrails.

Experiment types (risk escalation):

E0

Bid & Budget

Lowest risk. Auto-execute if score ≥70.

E1

Targeting

Medium risk. Auto-execute if score ≥70.

E2

Ad Copy

Medium risk, slower. Flagged for review.

E3

Structural

Highest risk. Always flagged for human decision.

Scoring formula (100 points total):

Expected Impact (30) + Confidence (25) + Reversibility (20) + Alignment (15) + Risk (10) = 100

Guardrails:

Max ±20% bid change · Max 25% budget shift · Circuit breaker at +30% CPA spike
